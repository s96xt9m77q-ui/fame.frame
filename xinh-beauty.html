Project Overview

Xinh Beauty came to me needing a brand identity built from the ground up — a visual language that balanced Vietnamese cultural nuance with contemporary luxury aesthetics. The challenge: create something that felt rooted yet modern, intimate yet aspirational, without falling into cliché.

Role

Lead Designer

Year

2023 – 2024

Sector

Tools

01 — The Challenge

Standing out in a saturated beauty market

The beauty industry is fiercely competitive — especially for indie brands. Xinh Beauty needed an identity strong enough to command attention on Instagram and TikTok, communicate luxury on a founder's budget, and authentically represent a Vietnamese-British dual cultural identity. A generic brand kit simply wasn't an option.

02 — Colour System

A palette drawn from silk and blossom

Inspired by the soft warmth of áo dài silk, lotus blossoms, and lacquerware — colours that signal femininity and refinement without resorting to millennial pink clichés.

03 — Social Media System

30+ templates built to scale

Every template was built in Canva Pro as a fully editable, team-friendly file — product reveals, testimonials, campaign launches, and story slides, all locked to the brand system.

04 — Full Brand Showcase

The Complete Identity & Print System

A comprehensive visual system bringing together print collateral, custom embossed stationery, luxurious product packaging, and a cohesive social grid layout. Embodying purity and prestige, each component is calibrated to reflect modern Vietnamese luxury.
