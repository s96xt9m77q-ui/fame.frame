Project Overview

Mexican Grill Co. needed to cut through a saturated London street food scene with bold visual identity and a cohesive social media presence. Beyond daily content, I also led the Red Nose Day 2026 charity campaign microsite — a branded fundraising activation layered onto their existing identity.

01 — The Challenge

Loud enough to stop the scroll

London's food scene is relentless. Mexican Grill Co. had great food but lacked the visual firepower to match it online. The brief was clear: create an identity bold enough to compete with established chains, feel authentically Mexican rather than Tex-Mex pastiche, and work across print, social media, and in-store environments simultaneously.

02 — Colour System

Heat, freshness, earth

The palette pulls directly from the Mexican landscape — terracotta clay, avocado lime, maize cream, and dark volcanic earth. Every colour has purpose: warmth, appetite, authenticity.
