Project Overview

Cougar Fashion is an independent UK label with a bold aesthetic and a founder-led vision. They needed a brand design system that felt truly premium — capable of sitting alongside established fashion houses on a feed — built on a founder's budget, without sacrificing any sophistication.

01 — The Challenge

Boutique budget, luxury presence

Independent fashion labels face an impossible tension: the expectation of premium aesthetics at a fraction of the budget of the brands they compete with for attention. The ask was to create a visual system that could hold its own against high-fashion editorial content — distinct, confident, and impossible to scroll past.

02 — Colour System

The power of restraint

A high-contrast monochrome foundation — near-black, pure white, warm silver — punctuated by an electric acid yellow that creates immediate, disruptive visual impact. No gradient softness. No compromise.

03 — Editorial Content

Fashion-editorial social system

A rolling social content system built for Instagram grid consistency — hero product shots, quote cards, campaign launches, and collection reveals, all tightly controlled within the brand grid.

03.1 — The Obsidian Edit (Noir)

A highly-structured, moody feed aesthetic. Highlights clean silhouettes, limited-edition drop counts, and luxury textured material close-ups alongside custom embossed packaging details.

03.2 — The Linen & Denim Edit

A bright, sun-drenched architectural showcase of relaxed apparel. Focuses on light linens, wash stories, fabric palettes, and relaxed silhouettes matching sustainable textile layouts.
