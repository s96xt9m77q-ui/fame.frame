Project Overview

Botánica Skincare is a plant-based, ethically sourced skincare brand navigating a crowded clean beauty market. They needed an identity that communicated genuine botanical credibility — not greenwashing — and a social media system that educated and inspired their community in equal measure.

Role

Lead Designer

Year

2023 – Present

Sector

Skincare / Wellness

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva Pro, Firefly

01 — The Challenge

Authentic sustainability in a greenwashed market

The clean beauty space is crowded with brands claiming sustainability without substance. Botánica needed a visual identity that felt genuinely rooted in nature — not performative — while remaining sophisticated enough to reach beyond the niche wellness audience. The design had to communicate trust, science, and beauty simultaneously.
