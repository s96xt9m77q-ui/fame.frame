Maison Noir

Artisanal Café & Coffee Roastery Identity

Client Maison Noir Paris
Role Lead Brand Identity Designer
Discipline Packaging, Collateral, Environmental Design
Year 2026

Maison Noir is a premium micro-roastery and artisanal coffee house located in the heart of Paris, France. The objective was to create a bold, high-contrast visual identity that respects traditional French bistro design while introducing a sharp, modern minimalist system suited for design-conscious consumers.

The visual system features a luxurious matte-black foundation contrasted by fine, warm-gold typography, monograms, and crest logos. The collateral rollout spans customized single-origin coffee packaging labels, gold-embossed takeaway cups, physical printed menu sheets, and spatial environmental branding for the micro-roastery shop.
