Melrose & Nolan

Artisanal Cafe & Coffee House Branding

Client Melrose & Nolan Cafe
Role Brand Identity Designer
Discipline Branding, Packaging, Signage
Year 2026

Melrose & Nolan is a premium artisanal cafe and bakery located in London. The visual identity project was commissioned to build a visual system reflecting their sophisticated, organic, and handcrafted culinary philosophy.

The brand system features a custom serif logo, bespoke coffee cup packaging, handcrafted paper bags, business stationery, and architectural store signage mockups. A rich navy, slate blue, and cream color palette gives a warm, upscale atmosphere to the entire cafe experience.
